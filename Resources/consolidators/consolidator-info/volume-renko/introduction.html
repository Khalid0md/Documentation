Volume Renko consolidators aggregate bars based on a fixed volume traded. The VolumeRenkoConsolidator produces Volume Renko bars every preset number of shares of the security being traded. A VolumeRenkoConsolidator with a bar size of 10000 produces a new bar by every 10000 shares being traded. If the volume jumps in more than 2 step sizes (i.e. >20000), the VolumeRenkoConsolidator produces multiple bars in a single time step.
